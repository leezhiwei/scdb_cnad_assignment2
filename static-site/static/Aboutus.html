ABOUT US

Welcome to SDCB, where we use innovative technology to empower seniors in managing their health and well-being from the comfort of their own homes. Our platform is designed to provide a user-friendly and supportive environment for elderly individuals to perform preliminary self-assessments and highlight vulnerable cases for further clinical attention.

We offer a range of tailored features to make health management simple and accessible for seniors. Our injury assessment tool allows users to take pictures of injuries or pain areas, receiving instant suggestions and guidance on whether a doctor's consultation is needed. The well-being assessment forms help seniors share their current health and discomfort levels, providing personalized results for better understanding.

For urgent needs, we provide an emergency contact and call option, enabling immediate help during critical situations. Our audio and video call feature, complete with AI-generated transcripts and summaries, ensures seamless communication between seniors and doctors for regular check-ups or urgent consultations.

We also include a Q&A section, where seniors can ask questions about their health or our platform, with answers provided by AI or medical professionals. Through the health advisement feature, users can directly contact doctors for advice on any health or medication-related concerns.

To promote overall well-being, our health guide offers curated content in audio, video, and image formats, with smart recommendations based on individual history. At SDCB, our goal is to provide seniors with the tools and support they need to take charge of their health, ensuring they feel cared for, connected, and informed every step of the way.

Your health and happiness are our priority. Let us help you live healthier, happier, and more confidently.
